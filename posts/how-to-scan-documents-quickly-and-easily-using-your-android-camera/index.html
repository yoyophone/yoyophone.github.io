1. Introduction to Document Scanning#

Document scanning is an important part of today’s digital office. After the document has been scanned, you’ll be presented with a preview. If you’re happy with it, hit the “Save” button. This will save the document to your device’s storage. You can then access the document from within the app or transfer it to another device.

Document scanning can be an extremely useful tool, allowing you to digitize physical documents of any kind. With this guide, you should now know how to quickly and easily scan any document using your Android device.
