2. Steps to Transfer Data from Android to iPhone Using Cable#

Transferring WhatsApp data from Android to iPhone can be a challenge, especially if you are a first-time user. But do not worry, it is easy to do once you know the steps. Here’s a step by step guide on how to do it using a cable:

First, plug the USB cable of your Android device to your computer. Then, open the WhatsApp app on the Android device and go to Settings > Chats > Chat Backup. Click on “Back Up” to start creating the backup. Once the backup is complete, unplug the USB cable and plug it into your iPhone.

Second, open the WhatsApp app on your iPhone and go to Settings > Chats > Chat Backup. You should be prompted to restore the backup. Click on “Restore Backup” to continue. The transfer process will start automatically and it may take a few minutes to finish depending on how much data you have transferred.

Third, after the transfer process is finished, open WhatsApp on both devices. You should be able to see the same conversations, contacts, and group chats on the iPhone as you had on Android. If you have added a local backup, it should also be available on the iPhone.

Finally, make sure to delete the Android backup to free up space. You can do this by going to Settings > Chats > Chat Backup on your Android device and clicking on “Delete Backup”. This will ensure that your data is not exposed to any third-party services.

So, there you have it - a step by step guide on how to easily transfer WhatsApp data from Android to iPhone using a cable. All you have to do now is follow the steps given above and you should be good to go.
