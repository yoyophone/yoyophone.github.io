1. Explain what is a QR code#

A QR code or Quick Response code is a type of barcode that contains a matrix of dots. It can be read using a smartphone camera. It typically stores website URLs, simple text, contact information or other data. Scanning a QR code with a camera on an Android phone or tablet is an easy process that can be done in just a few steps.

First, open the Camera app on your Android device and point it steadily towards the QR code you want to scan. It must be in a well lit area or else you’ll have difficulty scanning.

Next, the Camera app will automatically recognize the QR code. A notification will appear at the top of the screen with the information stored in the QR code.

You can then tap on the notification to open the URL, simple text, contact or other data stored in the QR code. Sometimes, you may be asked to download a third party QR code app in order to access the data stored in the QR code.

Finally, if the QR code doesn’t contain any useful information or data, you can simply tap on the screen to close the scanning window. With these few steps, scanning QR codes with your Android phone or tablet should be a breeze.
