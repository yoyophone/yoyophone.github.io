First, plug the USB cable into the source device and then into the destination device. The devices should detect each other and prompt you to choose the type of connection that you want. Select Media Transfer for the connection choice, and the devices should verify the connection.

Fourth, go back to the source device. Select the file(s) that you want to transfer and paste the contents to the previously created folder on the destination device.

Fifth, unplug the USB cable from both devices when the transfer is complete. Your files should now be on the destination device. By following these steps, you should have no trouble transferring your files quickly and easily from Android to Android via cable.
