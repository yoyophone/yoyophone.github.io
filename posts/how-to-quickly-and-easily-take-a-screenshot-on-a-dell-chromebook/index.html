If screenshots are moved or deleted from the Downloads folder, they can also be found in your Google Drive. Just open up the Drive app and look in your My Drive folder.

Finally, if you ever want to share a screenshot, you can easily do so by locating the screenshot in your Files app, right-clicking on it, and then selecting the option to share it.
