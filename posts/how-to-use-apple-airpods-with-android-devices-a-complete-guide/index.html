To begin, open the Settings app on your Android device and navigate to the Bluetooth section. Make sure the Bluetooth toggle is on and select “Add New Device”. You will be prompted to put your AirPods in pairing mode – to do this, hold the “setup” button on the back of the AirPods case until the light starts blinking.

The AirPods should appear in the list of available devices. Select them and confirm the pairing. The AirPods should connect within a few seconds and you will see a notification to confirm the successful connection.

Now you’re ready to start using your AirPods with your Android device. To do this, open the music or video app of your choice and hit play. The audio should automatically start playing through your AirPods. To control the volume or switch to another device, open the Bluetooth settings on your Android device.

That’s all you need to do to connect AirPods to an Android device. You can now enjoy all the features of your AirPods, including hands-free calling and audio sharing, on your Android device.
