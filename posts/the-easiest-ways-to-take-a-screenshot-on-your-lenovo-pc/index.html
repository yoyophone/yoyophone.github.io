1. Understanding the Basics of Taking a Screenshot#

Taking a screenshot on your Lenovo PC is easy and fast. To capture the entire screen, simply hit the ‘PrtScr’ key on your keyboard, which is usually located above the ‘Insert’ key. This will copy the whole screen to your clipboard and you can then paste the image into an image editor. For more control over the screenshot, press the Windows key and ‘PrtScr’. This captures the active window and saves it to your clipboard. You can also create a screenshot via the Snipping tool, which allows you to capture a selected portion of your screen. Open the Snipping tool from the Windows start menu to get started. Pressing ‘Alt’ and ‘PrtScr’ will give you a screenshot of the active window which you can then paste into an editor. For Lenovo users with touchscreen laptops, the ‘Windows’ key and volume down buttons should take a screenshot. This will save the screenshot to your Pictures > Screenshots folder. Once you’ve taken the screenshot you can save it, share it, or print it directly from the file.
