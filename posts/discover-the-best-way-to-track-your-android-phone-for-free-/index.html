Q2: How do I enable Find My Device?

A2: To enable Find My Device, open the “Settings” app on your Android device and navigate to the “Security & Location” section. Here, you’ll find the “Find My Device” option. After tapping on it, enable the “Remotely locate this device” and “Allow remote lock and erase” options.

Q3: How do I track a lost Android phone?

A3: To track a lost Android phone, you must first enable the Find My Device feature (as described in the previous answer). Then, you can visit the Find My Device website and sign in with your Google Account. The website will display a map with the location of your Android device.

A4: No, you cannot track an Android phone without the owner knowing. For the Find My Device feature to work, the owner must have enabled the feature and logged into their Google Account.
