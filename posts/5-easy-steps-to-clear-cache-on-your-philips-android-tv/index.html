Unhappy with your Philips Android TV performance? Clear your cache and get it running like new again with these 5 simple steps! Do you want to clear the cache on your Philips Android TV? A lot of people struggle with this issue and it can be quite daunting, but don’t worry! It’s actually pretty simple to clear the cache on your Philips Android TV. Here’s a quick guide on 5 Easy Steps to Clear Cache on Your Philips Android TV.

First, open the Settings menu by using your remote control. Then, go to the General tab and select Storage & Reset. Scroll down and click on Clear Cache. It’s that easy! Keep in mind that depending on the size of the cache, it may take a few seconds to completely clear the cache.

Second, you can also clear the cache from individual apps. To do so, open the App Management tab in the Settings section. Select an app, and then click on Clear Cache. You can repeat this step for all the apps you wish to clear the cache for.

Third, if you ever run into any problems when clearing the cache from individual apps, you can try using the apps Reset option. This will reset all the settings for the app back to its default.

Fourth, if you want a more thorough way to clear the cache on your Philips Android TV, you can always clear the entire system cache. To do so, go the Storage & Reset tab and select Clear System Cache. This will clear all the system caches including any cached data from the apps installed on your Philips Android TV.

Finally, if you ever experience your Philips Android TV having slower performance due to clogged caches, you can always try a factory reset. This will delete all the data stored on the Phillips Android TV and reinstall the operating system. Keep in mind that you will lose all your apps and data if you do a factory reset, so be sure to back up your data before doing a factory reset.

Overall, it’s easy to clear the cache on your Philips Android TV. It may take a few minutes to complete, but it’s sure worth it when you experience a speedier Philips Android TV!
