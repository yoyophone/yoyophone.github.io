Firstly, open the Settings menu and look for the Advanced option. Often times this will be found under the Systems & Updates tab. Here, find the Screen Recording option and enable it.

Next, open the Phone Manager app and switch to the Album tab. Tap on the Screen Recording option and adjust the recording settings. Finally, users can start the recording process through the Phone Manager app. Tap on the Start button and wait for the countdown. Once the recording has started, the Phone Manager app will appear on the screen. To pause the recording, simply press the pause icon.
