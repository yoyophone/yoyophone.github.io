To help make the process smoother, here’s a step-by-step guide on factory resetting your Android phone. First, open the Settings app on your device. Scroll down and select the System option. Tap on Reset and then Erase all data (Factory Reset). You will then be prompted to confirm you want to erase all data. Once you confirm, your phone will start to reset and the process may take a few minutes. Lastly, once the process is complete, your phone will be restored to factory settings.

A factory reset should be used as a last resort if your Android device is having issues or you simply want to start fresh. After the reset is complete, you can proceed with setting up your phone again and personalizing it with your preferences.
