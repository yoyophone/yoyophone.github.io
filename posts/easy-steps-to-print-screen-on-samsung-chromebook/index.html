1. How to Capture Screenshot on Samsung Chromebook with Keyboard Shortcut#

Capturing screenshots on a Samsung Chromebook is a great way to save content for later. It’s important to learn how to do it quickly with a keyboard shortcut. Here are the easy steps to do an on-screen print screen on a Samsung Chromebook:

Press the ‘Ctrl’ and ‘Window Switcher’ buttons at the same time on your Chromebook keyboard. This will capture the whole screen of your Chromebook. The screenshot will be automatically saved in your Downloads folder.

Alternatively, you can press ‘Ctrl’, ‘Shift’ and ‘Window Switcher’ buttons together to take a screenshot of a particular area. After pressing these 3 keys, your cursor will change into a crosshair. Drag the crosshair over the area that needs to be captured and it will automatically take a screenshot.

You can also take a screenshot of just the active window by pressing ‘Ctrl’ and ‘Alt’ plus the ‘Window Switcher’ keys together. This will capture only the current window and not the whole screen. The screenshot will again be stored in the “Downloads” folder of your Chromebook.

These are the simple steps to take screenshots on a Samsung Chromebook with keyboard shortcuts. You can now capture content quickly and easily with these easy methods.
