Q1: What is Apple TV?

A1: Apple TV is a streaming platform developed by Apple Inc. that lets users watch movies, TV shows, and various other content on their Apple device or on third-party TVs and streaming devices.

Q2: What devices are compatible with Apple TV?

A2: Apple TV is compatible with various Apple devices, including iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple TV (4th generation or later). It can also be accessed on select third-party TV and streaming devices, such as select smart TVs, Roku devices, Amazon Fire TV devices, and Chromecast.

Q3: What are the advantages of using Apple TV?

A3: One of the advantages of using Apple TV is its integration with iCloud, allowing users to easily access their TV shows, movies, and other media from across their devices.
