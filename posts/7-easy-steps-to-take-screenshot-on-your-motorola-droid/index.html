Tired of struggling to take screenshot on your Motorola Droid? Don’t worry, here we provide you with 7 simple steps to guide you on how to take daily screenshots on your Motorola Droid device easily! The Motorola Droid has a wealth of functionality to it, and one of the most useful functions is being able to quickly take screenshots of whatever is on your screen. Taking a screenshot on your Motorola Droid is quite simple, and with this article, you will understand exactly how to do it in just 7 easy steps.

Step 1: Begin by using two fingers to press the Volume Down and Power Buttons simultaneously. This will cause your phone to vibrate and you should see the screenshot appear as a preview in the lower right corner of your screen.

Step 2: If you want to save the screenshot, click the preview icon. The screenshot will then be saved in the Gallery app.

Step 3: If you want to share the screenshot instead, click the “Share” button. This will open a menu asking you what you’d like to do with the screenshot. You can post it in social media, attach it to an email or text message, or send it to another app.

Step 4: If you want to take another screenshot, simply press the Volume Down and Power Buttons again.

Step 5: If you want to take multiple screenshots one after the other without having to press the buttons each time, press the Volume Down and Power Buttons simultaneously for two seconds. This will activate a brief screenshot animation and every time you tap the screen, the current screenshot will be saved.

Step 6: When you have taken all of your screenshots, press the Volume Down and Power Buttons to deactivate the multi-screenshot animation.

Step 7: You can find all of your screenshots in your Gallery app.

Taking screenshots on your Motorola Droid is now easier than ever! With just 7 easy steps, you can capture images quickly and share them with the world.
