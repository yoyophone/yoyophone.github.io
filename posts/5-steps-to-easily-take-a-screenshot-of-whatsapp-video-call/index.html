1. Taking a Screenshot of Whatsapp Video Call on iOS Devices#

Third, iPhone X and later models use a different set of commands. To take a screenshot, press and quickly release the lock and volume up buttons. Again, you should hear a shutter sound and the screenshot will be stored in the Photos app.

Finally, it is important to note that no matter which method you use, the screenshot will be saved to the “All Photos” album in the Photos app. After taking the screenshot, you can use your favourite image editing app to edit, crop, and share the image.
