To turn off VVM, open settings and find the “Phone” app. Tap on “VVM” and select “Disable”. This will stop visual voicemail notifications from popping up on your phone. You can also delete stored Visual Voicemail messages from here. Simply tap the “Delete” button and confirm the action to permanently remove voicemails from your device. Finally, if you want to re-enable Visual Voicemail, simply follow the same steps and select “Enable”. After that, you’ll start receiving notifications for new voicemails.
