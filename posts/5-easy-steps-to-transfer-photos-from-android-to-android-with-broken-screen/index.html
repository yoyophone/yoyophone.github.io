1. Preparation#

Transferring photos from Android to Android can be a tricky task, especially if one of the device has a broken screen. Fear not, as here is a quick guide on how to do it. The following are five easy steps to transfer photos from an Android device with a broken screen to another Android:

First, plug the Android phone with the broken screen into a laptop or PC. Enable USB Debugging on the device, which can be done in Settings > Developer Options > USB Debugging. Second, open the command prompt on the laptop. Next, type in adb devices to check if the device is detected. After that, type in adb pull /sdcard/DCIM/(folder name)/ and the photos will begin transferring. Lastly, when the transfer is completed, unplug the device and plug in the other Android device. The transferred photos will be in the same folder on the new device.

Following these five steps, users can easily transfer their photos from one Android device to another, even when one device has a broken screen.
