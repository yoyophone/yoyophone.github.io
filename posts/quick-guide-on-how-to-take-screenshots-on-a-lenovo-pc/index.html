2. Saving & Sharing your Screenshot#

If you are using a Lenovo PC laptop, it’s very easy to take a screenshot. All you need to do is press the Windows Logo button + Prt Sc button. This will take a screenshot of your entire screen, and it will be saved to your laptop’s Pictures Folder.

You can also choose to take a partial screenshot. To do this, press the Windows Logo button + Shift + S. This will bring up the Snipping Tool, which allows you to draw a rectangle around the area of the screen you want to capture.

Once you’ve taken the screenshot, you can easily share or save it. To quickly share the screenshot, press the Windows Logo button + V. This will open the Windows Share UI, where you can select the app you’d like to share it with.

To save the screenshot, open the Pictures folder (or wherever you’ve saved the screenshot to), right-click on the image, and select Save as. This will open a dialog box where you can select the file format and file name. Once you’ve filled these out, click Save to save the screenshot.
