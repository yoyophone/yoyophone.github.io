2. Disable Voicemail Notification for Specific Contact#

If you have been getting annoying voicemail notifications from a specific contact, chances are you want to know how to disable voicemail notifications for that contact. Luckily, it’s very easy to do on your Android phone. Here’s a simple step-by-step guide to clearing voicemail notifications on your Android phone.

The first step is to enter your contact list to find the specific contact you want to disable voicemail notifications for. Once you’ve found that contact, open their information page and tap the three-dot menu icon located at the top of the page. A list of options will appear. Select ‘Notifications’, and scroll down to the ‘Voicemails’ option. Uncheck the box next to ‘Caller notifications’, and you’re done.

The next step is to turn off the voicemail notifications for the contact. To do this, open the phone app and select ‘Voicemail’. Tap the three-dot menu icon at the top of the page, and select ‘Settings’. Uncheck the box next to ‘Voicemail notifications’ for the contact.

Finally, you can also turn off other notifications for the contact on your Android phone. To do this, open the Phone app, and select ‘Call Settings’. Tap ‘Notifications’ and uncheck all the boxes that appear. This will turn off all notifications from the contact.

By following these steps, you can easily disable voicemail notifications for a specific contact on your Android phone. This will ensure you don’t get any pesky voicemails from that contact, making it easier for you to stay focused on your tasks.

Q1. How do I clear voicemail notifications on my Android phone?

A1. You can clear voicemail notifications on your Android phone by following these steps: open the voicemail application on your phone, tap the voicemail notification, tap the three-dot menu icon in the top-right corner of the voicemail screen, select ‘Delete’ and then tap ‘OK’ to confirm.

Q2. What should I do if the three-dot menu icon is not visible?

A2. If the three-dot menu icon is not visible, you can try swiping left or right on the voicemail notification. This should bring up the menu. If this does not work, try resetting the voicemail app.

Q3. How do I reset the voicemail app?

A3. To reset the voicemail app, go to the settings menu on your Android phone, select ‘Applications’, tap ‘Voicemail’, select ‘Uninstall’ and then tap ‘OK’ to confirm. This will uninstall the app and remove any voicemail notifications.

Q4. Is it possible to disable voicemail notifications on my Android phone?

A4. Yes, you can disable voicemail notifications on your Android phone by going to the settings menu, selecting ‘Applications’, tapping ‘Voicemail’, selecting ‘Notifications’ and then unchecking the ‘Show notifications’ option.

Q5. What should I do if deleting voicemail notifications does not work?

A5. If deleting voicemail notifications does not work, try restarting your Android phone and then go through the steps listed above. If this does not work, you can try hard resetting your device or contacting your phone’s manufacturer for further assistance.
