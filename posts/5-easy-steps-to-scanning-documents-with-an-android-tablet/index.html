2. Extract the Text from the Document#

If you’re looking for an easy way to extract text from documents using a tablet, this article will provide a quick overview on how to do it. It’s a simple 5-step process that can be done quickly and easily.

Second, open the document and use the app’s scan feature to capture an image of the text. Depending on the app you may be able to adjust the brightness or contrast of the image to make the text easier to read.

Third, use the OCR (Optical Character Recognition) feature to convert the scanned image into text. This feature typically works well and is reasonably accurate.

Fourth, once the text appears you can select, copy and paste it into any other app. Additionally, the app may allow you to save the text-based document for later use.

Finally, save the text document or print it out to store it elsewhere if needed.

Q1. What are the steps to scanning documents with an Android tablet?

A1. Scanning documents with an Android tablet is a simple process that only requires a few steps. Here are the five steps to follow:

  1. Install a scanning app – There are many scanning apps available on the Google Play Store. Pick one that suits your needs and install it.

  2. Open the scanning app – After it’s installed, open the scanning app. Choose the scan type and configure the settings if needed.

  3. Take a photo – Place the document you need to scan in a well-lit area and take a photo of it.

  4. Preview and adjust – Check the scan to ensure it’s clear and has all the information you need. If not, retake the photo or make adjustments as needed.

Q2. Are there any documents that cannot be scanned with an Android tablet?

A2. Generally, any document that can be photographed can be scanned with an Android tablet. However, if your document is larger than the scanning app’s image size limits, you may have to split it into multiple scans or get a separate scanning device.

Q3. What type of scanning is available on an Android tablet?

A3. Scanning on an Android tablet depends on the app you’re using. Most scanning apps offer different types of scans such as document scanning, business card scanning, and invoice scanning.

Q4. Is it safe to save scanned documents on my device?

A4. Yes, it’s usually safe to save scanned documents on your device. Make sure you’ve enabled encryption and password protection if you’re concerned about document security.
