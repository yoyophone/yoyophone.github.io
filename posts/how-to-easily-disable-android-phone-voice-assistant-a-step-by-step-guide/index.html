1. Disabling Voice Assistants#

Many of us are familiar with voice assistants like Google Assistant and Siri in our Android phones. While these can be useful at times, it can be quite annoying when they start speaking out of nowhere. Fortunately, it is possible to completely turn off voice assistants on Android phones. All it takes is a few simple steps. First, open the “Settings” app on your Android phone and select “Apps and Notifications”. Then, select “Application Manager” and search for “Voice Assistants”. Once this is done, you can tap on the “Disable” button to turn it off. Finally, you will need to restart your phone for the changes to take effect. After that, your Android phone will no longer have voice assistants activated.

In conclusion, turning off the voice assistant on your Android phone is easy to do. All you have to do is open up the Settings, go to the Apps tab, select the voice assistant and toggle it off.
